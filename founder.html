The Founder

Seakwa Jonas Mochebane (PREASX24)

Professional Overview

Seakwa Jonas Mochebane, known online as PREASX24, is the founder of DTECH, a South African platform focused on structured, practical digital systems designed to solve real-world problems in academic and community environments. The journey began around 2018, with early tools and utilities built to support students and learners, and DTECH was formally classified in 2020 through the launch of the first platform on Facebook.

DTECH emphasizes clarity, usability, and long-term scalability. Each platform is designed to be practical and adaptable, capable of evolving alongside the users and communities it serves. The focus is on creating reliable infrastructure that performs consistently and delivers tangible value, rather than temporary solutions or fleeting trends.

A central philosophy at DTECH is leveraging freely available systems and services to develop platforms that are user-friendly, accessible, and free to use for the general public. By creatively combining open tools, free hosting services, and structured design principles, new platforms are built quickly, efficiently, and sustainably, without compromising quality or functionality. This approach ensures that digital innovation remains open and inclusive, offering resources that anyone can access and benefit from.

The DTECH ecosystem spans multiple domains — from digital marketplaces to academic support tools, utility scripts, and system infrastructure — all designed with usability, maintainability, and accessibility in mind. Platforms are community-oriented, supporting students, creators, and learners while promoting knowledge sharing, practical problem solving, and long-term engagement.

Innovation at DTECH is grounded in real-world needs. Every system prioritizes structured architecture, scalability, and user-focused design, making it easy for people to interact with the platform, understand its functions, and achieve meaningful results. Solutions are practical, approachable, and tailored to empower users to navigate the digital landscape confidently.

Since its inception, DTECH has remained committed to empowering learners, creators, and communities by building technology that works for them. By prioritizing structured systems, scalable infrastructure, and public accessibility, DTECH continues to lay the foundation for impactful digital innovation in South Africa, creating tools and experiences that are practical, reliable, and inclusive.
